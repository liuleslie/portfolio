Litebub: Quick Chat Setup
Winter 2020
Interface Design
Desktop prototype
Timeline: 1 week
Tools: Figma

Background

As part of a team of four at Interhackt, a design hackathon, I drafted sketches for a lightweight chatroom application called Litebub. Litebub gathers people in the same comment section—or Twitter thread—and serves as a temporary discussion space.

Concepting + Results

We wanted to focus on the energy and flow of a conversation. Inspired by the app architecture of sites like Reddit, we strove to bake in a simple feature to allow for more specific and private side conversations. The avatars, moving along the chat bubbles that squish and stretch as messages take form, suggest the feeling of racing to speak.
